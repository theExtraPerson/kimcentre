Welcome to Kampala International Medical Centre

Providing compassionate care and medical excellence to the community of Kampala and beyond.

Established in 2005, Kampala International Medical Centre (KIMC) has grown from a small clinic to a comprehensive healthcare facility serving thousands of patients annually. Our state-of-the-art facility combines modern medical technology with a patient-centered approach to deliver exceptional healthcare services.

At KIMC, we believe that quality healthcare should be accessible to all. Our team of experienced healthcare professionals is committed to providing personalized care that addresses the unique needs of each patient. Whether you're seeking preventive care, treatment for an acute condition, or management of a chronic illness, we're here to support your health journey every step of the way.

Our Journey

2005: The Beginning

KIMC was founded as a small outpatient clinic with just five staff members, dedicated to serving the local community with basic healthcare services.

2010: Expansion of Services

We expanded our facility to include specialized departments for maternal health, pediatrics, and emergency care, significantly increasing our capacity to serve patients.

2015: Technological Advancement

KIMC embraced digital health technology, implementing electronic medical records and telemedicine services to enhance patient care and accessibility.

2020: Comprehensive Care Center

We completed a major expansion, adding new departments including dental care, mental health services, and advanced diagnostic imaging, transforming KIMC into a comprehensive healthcare center.

Today: Looking to the Future

KIMC continues to grow and evolve, constantly seeking innovative ways to improve patient care and expand our services to meet the changing healthcare needs of our community.

Our Vision and Mission

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare provider in East Africa, recognized for excellence in patient care, innovation in medical practice, and commitment to community well-being.

We envision a future where quality healthcare is accessible to all, preventive care is prioritized, and our community enjoys optimal health and wellness.

Our Mission

To deliver compassionate, high-quality healthcare services that improve the lives of our patients and communities through:

  • Patient-centered care that respects individual needs and preferences
  • Evidence-based medical practices and continuous quality improvement

  • Community engagement and health education initiatives

Our Core Services

At KIMC, we focus on providing comprehensive healthcare through our specialized core services that address the diverse needs of our patients.

Digital Health

Our digital health services leverage cutting-edge technology to enhance patient care and accessibility. We understand that modern healthcare extends beyond the walls of our facility, which is why we've invested in comprehensive digital solutions.

Key Features:

  • Telemedicine consultations with our specialists
  • Secure electronic medical records accessible to patients
  • Mobile health monitoring for chronic conditions
  • Online appointment scheduling and prescription refills
  • Remote patient monitoring for post-discharge care

Our digital health platform ensures that quality healthcare is accessible to all patients, regardless of location or mobility constraints, while maintaining the highest standards of care and data security.

Dental Care

Our comprehensive dental care department is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by experienced dental professionals. We recognize that oral health is integral to overall wellbeing and offer a full spectrum of dental services.

Services Include:

  • Preventive dentistry (cleanings, check-ups, fluoride treatments)
  • Restorative procedures (fillings, crowns, bridges)
  • Cosmetic dentistry (whitening, veneers, smile makeovers)
  • Pediatric dental care
  • Oral surgery and implant dentistry
  • Emergency dental services

Our dental team focuses on patient comfort and education, ensuring that each visit contributes to long-term oral health and a confident smile.

Mental & Psycho-Social Support

Our mental health and psycho-social support services address the emotional, psychological, and social aspects of health. We believe in a holistic approach to healthcare that recognizes the profound connection between mental and physical wellbeing.

Our Approach:

  • Individual and group counseling sessions
  • Psychiatric evaluation and medication management
  • Cognitive behavioral therapy and other evidence-based treatments
  • Stress management and resilience building
  • Support for trauma, grief, and life transitions
  • Community mental health education and outreach

Our compassionate mental health team creates a safe, stigma-free environment where patients can address their mental health needs with dignity and receive personalized care for optimal emotional wellbeing.
