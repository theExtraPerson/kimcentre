Comprehensive Healthcare Solutions

At Kampala International Medical Centre, we offer a wide range of medical services designed to meet the diverse healthcare needs of our community. Our team of experienced healthcare professionals is committed to providing high-quality, patient-centered care using state-of-the-art equipment and facilities.

General Medicine

Comprehensive healthcare for patients of all ages

Our general medicine department provides primary healthcare services for patients of all ages. We focus on preventive care, diagnosis, and treatment of various medical conditions, as well as management of chronic diseases.

Services Include:

  • Routine check-ups and physical examinations
  • Diagnosis and treatment of common illnesses
  • Management of chronic conditions like diabetes and hypertension
  • Immunizations and vaccinations
  • Health education and counseling
Book an Appointment

Home-based Care

Professional medical care in the comfort of your home

Our home-based care services bring quality healthcare to patients who are unable to visit our facility due to mobility issues, chronic conditions, or post-surgical recovery needs. We provide personalized care plans delivered by trained healthcare professionals.

Services Include:

  • Skilled nursing care
  • Physical therapy and rehabilitation
  • Medication management
  • Wound care and dressing changes
  • Palliative and end-of-life care
Request Home Care

Psycho-Social Support

Comprehensive mental health and emotional wellbeing services

Our psycho-social support services address the mental, emotional, and social aspects of health. We provide counseling, therapy, and support for individuals dealing with mental health conditions, emotional challenges, or difficult life transitions.

Services Include:

  • Individual and group counseling
  • Stress management and coping strategies
  • Depression and anxiety treatment
  • Trauma counseling and PTSD support
  • Family therapy and relationship counseling
Schedule a Consultation

Ultra-Sound Scan & Diagnostics

Advanced imaging and diagnostic services

Our diagnostic department is equipped with state-of-the-art ultrasound technology to provide accurate imaging for diagnosis and monitoring of various conditions. Our experienced technicians and radiologists ensure high-quality images and precise interpretations.

Services Include:

  • Obstetric ultrasound (pregnancy scans)
  • Abdominal ultrasound
  • Pelvic ultrasound
  • Cardiac ultrasound (echocardiogram)
  • Vascular ultrasound
Book a Scan

Emergency Services

24/7 emergency care when you need it most

Our emergency department is staffed around the clock to provide immediate care for critical and life-threatening conditions. We have a rapid response ambulance service and a team of emergency medicine specialists ready to handle any medical emergency.

Services Include:

  • 24/7 emergency room services
  • Rapid response ambulance service
  • Trauma care
  • Critical care and stabilization
  • Emergency surgeries
